APPROVEFY – BASIC vs PRO PLAN FEATURES
=======================================
Use this when creating/editing plans in Shopify Partner Dashboard (Managed Pricing).
Copy the feature lists into your plan descriptions or listing.

-----------------------------------------
BASIC PLAN
-----------------------------------------
Monthly Price: Lower tier

FEATURES:
• Customer Registrations: Up to 100 / month
• Customer Approval System: Manual approval
• Customer Status Management: Pending / Approved / Denied
• Customer Search & Filters: Basic search
• Customer Analytics Dashboard: Basic metrics
• CSV Export: Not included
• B2B Registration Form Builder: 1 form
• Multi-Step Registration Forms: Not included
• Drag & Drop Form Builder: Yes
• Custom Fields: Limited fields
• File Upload Fields: Not included
• Newsletter Opt-In Detection: Not included
• Default Form Selection: Not included
• Form Cloning: Not included
• Form Translations / Multi-Language: Not included
• Theme Styling Controls: Basic styling
• Email Templates (Approval / Rejection): Basic templates
• Email Branding (Logo, Colors): Not included
• Approval Email with Activation Link: Not included
• SMTP Email Support: Not included
• Resend Approval / Rejection Emails: Not included
• Customer Detail Editing: Basic fields
• Shopify Customer Tags: 1 approved tag
• Shopify Address Sync: Not included
• Delete Customer Options: Not included
• Priority Support: Not included

-----------------------------------------
PRO PLAN
-----------------------------------------
Monthly Price: Higher tier

FEATURES:
• Customer Registrations: Unlimited
• Customer Approval System: Manual + Auto approval
• Customer Status Management: Pending / Approved / Denied
• Customer Search & Filters: Advanced search + filters
• Customer Analytics Dashboard: Advanced analytics
• CSV Export: Full export
• B2B Registration Form Builder: Unlimited forms
• Multi-Step Registration Forms: Included
• Drag & Drop Form Builder: Yes
• Custom Fields: Unlimited fields
• File Upload Fields: Included
• Newsletter Opt-In Detection: Included
• Default Form Selection: Included
• Form Cloning: Included
• Form Translations / Multi-Language: Included
• Theme Styling Controls: Advanced styling + Custom CSS
• Email Templates (Approval / Rejection): Fully customizable templates
• Email Branding (Logo, Colors): Included
• Approval Email with Activation Link: Included
• SMTP Email Support: Custom SMTP
• Resend Approval / Rejection Emails: Included
• Customer Detail Editing: Full editing + sync with Shopify
• Shopify Customer Tags: Multiple tags
• Shopify Address Sync: Included
• Delete Customer Options: Delete from Shopify / App / Both
• Priority Support: Included
