# Approvefy — Pricing documentation

**Last updated:** May 9, 2026  
**Support:** approvefy@xloxi.com  
**Website:** https://approvefy.xloxi.com/

This document describes how Approvefy **plans**, **pricing**, and **included features** are presented in the product. Charges are billed through **Shopify** according to what you approve at subscription time in the Shopify admin or App Store flow.

---

## Overview

Approvefy offers three subscription tiers:

| Plan | Monthly price (USD) | Typical use |
|------|---------------------|-------------|
| **Basic** | $4.99 / month | Getting started with registration and approvals (auto-only), starter field types |
| **Standard** | $14.99 / month | Full registration builder, manual + auto approval, languages, SMTP, email templates |
| **Premium** | $24.99 / month | Everything in Standard plus file uploads, webhooks/privacy tooling, priority support roadmap |

Pricing is shown on the **Pricing** screen inside the embedded app (**`/app/pricing`**) and may also appear on your **Shopify App Store** listing—if anything differs between this document and your live storefront or checkout, treat the **in-checkout or listing price** as the one that applies to your subscription.

---

## Trials and billing

- **Free trial:** New subscriptions may include a **7-day free trial** (as displayed at subscribe time). Exact trial length follows what Shopify shows when you approve the charge.
- **Billing:** Shopify bills the subscription to your Shopify account (recurring application charge).
- **Plan changes:** You can move between plans when offered in your billing flow—feature limits in the app follow the plan stored for your shop after Shopify confirms the new charge.
- **Taxes:** Any taxes depend on Shopify’s billing and your store’s location—see your Shopify invoices.

Questions about an invoice line item: contact **approvefy@xloxi.com** and include your **store domain**.

## Plan summaries (marketing copy aligned with product)

### Basic — $4.99 / month

- Good for stores starting with B2B-style registration **with auto approval**.
- Includes **starter** custom field types (not every advanced field type), **wholesale** registration form, theme app embed, Home setup/analytics/search/filters/bulk actions, CSV export, and customer support.

### Standard — $14.99 / month (“Most popular” in-app)

- **Unlimited form field types** (except items reserved for Premium, e.g. file upload).
- **Wholesale + multi-step** forms, multiple forms and templates, appearance/theme templates/CSS.
- **Email alerts, SMTP**, and editable approval/rejection email designs.
- **Manual and automatic** approval, multiple storefront languages, **approved customer tag** sync with Shopify, large lists and sizeable CSV exports, customer support.

### Premium — $24.99 / month

- **Everything in Standard**, plus **file upload** fields (unlimited storage as implemented in-product).
- **Webhooks** and **store privacy / data-related** tooling as implemented for Premium merchants.
- **Priority support**, onboarding assistance, early access to new features as offered.

## Basic plan — field types (technical note)

On **Basic**, the form builder only allows **starter** field categories (names, email/password, phone, company, core address-style fields, text/textarea/small structural fields). Advanced types (examples: dropdowns, dates, uploads, richer blocks) unlock on **Standard and Premium** unless the product UI states otherwise—always follow what the picker shows for your store’s active plan.

**File uploads** unlock only on **Premium**.
